## Who is the customer?
Evergreen Quote is designed for individuals and families who need a quick estimate for property insurance coverage and want to compare options before speaking with an insurance agent. These customers are often researching insurance online, looking for a fast and simple way to understand potential coverage and pricing. Today, many of them rely on phone calls, email exchanges, or lengthy insurance websites to get basic quote information.

## What pain does Evergreen Quote remove?
Customers are frustrated by having to spend significant time answering repetitive questions, waiting for callbacks, or navigating complicated insurance forms just to understand what their coverage might cost. Evergreen Quote removes the pain of a slow and confusing quote process by providing a simple, self-service way to get an insurance estimate quickly and clearly.
